AL-RASHED TRADING CENTER(Trading & Traveling Co.)

It is our pleasure in introducing ourselves as one of the oldest leading trading establishment that has been engaged in the business sector of trading and traveling in Yemen. This family company was founded in the early years of the sixties of the last century in the city of Aden, it was one of the first to represent well known world brands by importing all electrical and electronics line of products. The company was considered one of the very few importers and exporters in Yemen at that time. Then the company expanded its activities and was expanded in the fields of industry, travel agencies and tourism, leading to more success for the group. The group also has established sub-companies under its umbrella and opened more branches across the country (South and North Yemen). AL-RASHED TRADING CENTER (A.R.T.C) is one the Sub-companies that works under the Group which founded in 1993. A.R.T.C. represents many respective suppliers in Electrical, Electronics, HVAC, Security Products and ...etc. for the Market of Yemen. Due to such activity in sales, the group founded maintenance workshops, Spare Parts and take care of after sale services for all products, and took into consideration the investing in its Employees and engineers by sending them for training programs to Europe, East Asia and the Middle East, Locally or by attending courses sponsored by companies training programs. Indeed, that investment was an added value to our business. The company own state of the Art machines for Duct Fabrication, grilles and ...etc.
